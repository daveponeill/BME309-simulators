How to read this

S-plane: The imaginary axis (jω) is the stability boundary. Poles in the left half-plane (σ < 0) give stable filters. The natural frequency ωn = √(σ²+ω²) sets the corner frequency in the Bode plot.

Bode magnitude: Each pole contributes −20 dB/decade slope above its corner frequency; each zero adds +20 dB/decade. Complex conjugate pairs contribute ±40 dB/decade.

Asymptotic approximation: The dashed curve shows the straight-line approximation — flat below the corner frequency, then sloped above. Real filters deviate near the corner by up to ±3 dB (poles) or ±3 dB (zeros).
